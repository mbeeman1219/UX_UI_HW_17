I am an Arizona-based Filipina-Navajo user experience researcher. I have lived in Arizona all my life, and I graduated from Arizona State University with a degree in Biology. I originally was on track to become a physician’s assistant until I realized I wanted to use my medical background to help patients and providers in a different way other than patient care. So, I went on to learn about UX/UI at the University of Denver.

Due to my science background, I have always been someone who is curious about the human mind and learning about how people think and why they do it in the way that they do. Then, I learned to combine my love of logic and psychology with the creative aspect of the user experience and meshed them together into a new path in my life centered around user research.

When I’m not trying to delve into user research, you can find me trying new restaurants, watching anime, playing video games, or napping with my cat.
